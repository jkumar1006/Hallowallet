## Tips for Best Results

### 1. Keep Transactions Updated
- Add expenses as they happen
- Use voice assistant for quick entry
- Review weekly to stay on track

### 2. Be Realistic with Goals
- Set achievable savings targets
- Adjust if circumstances change
- Don't set goals too high or too low

### 3. Use the Alternatives
- Click the Amazon links
- Compare features and reviews
- Often find better deals

### 4. Check Before Every Purchase
- Make it a habit
- Prevents impulse buying
- Helps reach savings goals

### 5. Review Monthly
- Check your progress at month-end
- Adjust spending if needed
- Celebrate when on track!

## Common Questions

**Q: Why does it say "Reconsider" when I have money in my account?**
A: The system considers your savings goal, not just your bank balance. It helps you save for the future, not just spend what you have.

**Q: Can I override the recommendation?**
A: Yes! These are suggestions to help you make informed decisions. You always have the final say.

**Q: How accurate are the cheaper alternatives?**
A: The system suggests similar products at lower price points. Always check reviews and specifications on Amazon before buying.

**Q: What if I don't have any transactions yet?**
A: Add some expenses first! The system needs data to give accurate recommendations. Start by adding your regular expenses (rent, groceries, utilities).
